Welcome To The Absence Tracking System

The Absence Tracking System (ATS) is a program to facilitate the management and documentation of Annual Leave and Absences

To get started, click on your name on the top right of this page and select an option. Below is a descripton of all the features available to you, please click on the section to view more details. You can return to this page at any time by clicking on the Home Icon. This page displays your current Annual Leave Balance, Department Calendar and Request History. You can request new Annual Leave on this page and view statistics on your Annual Leave. This page displays a record of your Absences. Absences which have been entered by your Line Manager, require your confirmation. Any Absences waiting to be confirmed will be displayed on this page. A history of your Absences can be viewed by selecting "View" then "Absence History". Statistics on your Absences can be viewd by selecting "View" then "Absence Statistics". This page is only available to Managers. On this page you can authorise or decline Employees Annual Leave Requests. View a Department Calendar. View a break down of your Departments Annual Leave by selecting "View" then "Department Statistics". This page is only available to Managers. On this page you can view your Departments Absence History. To enter a new Absence for an employee, select "Create" then "Absence". To view the Absence of a particular employee, select "View" then "Employee Absence". To view the Absence Statistics of the whole department, select "View" then "Department Statistics". This feature is available only to those Users who have been granted System Admininstrator Access. On this page you can create new Departments and employees to be added to the system. You can also view all the employees and departments currently entered into the system by selecting, "Browse" then either "Department" or "Employee".
